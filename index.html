Cozy Move is a perfect tool for the people who wants to relocate to a different place. It will give you the personalized results based on your family's characteristics. Assume you have a family of two children and a spouse. You all have different characteristics. You have a job, your spouse loves to ride a bike and your children should attend to school. Using Cozy Move, you have a chance to define the requirements for each individual and application will show the different options for you ordered by suitability. You can also use the application as a person living alone. Just select where do you want to live and enter your expectations, application will take care of the rest and give you the personalized results.

Cozy Move is also a perfect tool for homeowners. Once you added your house for rent/sale, tool will display the nearby locations to you. If there are missing important places you want to show off, you can add those immediately with location adding feature.Cozy Move will have two languages (English and Turkish) in its initial release. After that it will cover more languages. It will also show you the possible moving expenses based on your geolocation and places to order food based on the reviews of other people for the first couple nights if you wish. Ultimately, Cozy Move is a tool for people who wants to relocate without trouble. For more info, you can visit FAQ below or contact me directly.
